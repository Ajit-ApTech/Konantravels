Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How do I book a vehicle?

You can book a vehicle through multiple channels - via our website, mobile app, or by calling our customer support. Simply select your preferred vehicle, choose your dates and times, and complete the booking process with the required details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including cash, UPI, credit/debit cards, digital wallets (Paytm, Google Pay, PhonePe), and net banking. For advance bookings, you can also make online payments through our secure payment gateway.

Are drivers included in the price?

Yes, all our vehicles come with professional, experienced drivers unless you opt for a self-drive option. Our drivers are trained, verified, and wear proper uniforms.

Can I modify my booking?

Yes, you can modify your booking up to 2 hours before the scheduled pickup time, subject to vehicle availability. Please contact our customer support to make any changes to your booking.

What is your cancellation policy?

For cancellations made more than 24 hours before pickup: Full refund. For cancellations within 24 hours: 50% refund. For no-shows: No refund. Please note that cancellation charges may vary for special packages and corporate bookings.

Do you offer outstation services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive outstation travel services across major cities and tourist destinations. Our packages include driver allowance, fuel charges, and toll taxes. We also offer one-way and round-trip options.

How are the drivers verified?

All our drivers undergo a thorough verification process including background checks, driving license verification, and training programs. They are also required to wear uniforms and carry ID cards for your safety.

Are the vehicles air-conditioned?

Most of our vehicles come with air conditioning. You can filter vehicles by AC availability during booking. All our AC vehicles are regularly maintained for optimal comfort.

What if my flight is delayed?

We monitor flight schedules and provide free waiting time for delayed flights. For domestic flights, we offer 1 hour of free waiting time. For international flights, it's 2 hours. Beyond that, nominal charges may apply.

Do you offer corporate packages?

Yes, we offer special corporate packages with discounted rates, monthly billing options, and dedicated account managers. Contact our sales team to discuss your corporate travel requirements.

Is there a security deposit?

No security deposit is required for most bookings. For special vehicles, luxury cars, or long-term rentals, a refundable security deposit may be applicable, which will be clearly communicated during booking.

Still Have Questions?

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Reach out to our support team and we'll be happy to help.
